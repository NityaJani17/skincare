Skincare Tips

Expert Advice for Healthy and Beautiful Skin

Tip 1

Properly cleanse your skin to remove impurities and maintain a clear complexion. Use a gentle cleanser suited to your skin type.

Tip 2

Apply toner after cleansing to keep your skin even toned and hydrated. Applying only toner with moisturiser can also work.

Tip 3

Apply serums after cleansing according to your skincare routine and needs. Use niacinamide for acne. Hyaluronic acid is best suited for glowing skin.

Tip 4

Keep your skin hydrated and moisturized to prevent dryness. Moisturiser can also be directly applied after cleansing.

Tip 5

Always apply sunscreen with at least SPF 50 before going outdoors to protect your skin from harmful UV rays. Reapply your sunscreen after every 5-6 hours to see better results.

Tip 6

Only applying moisturiser with sunscreen also works wonders on your skin. Always following 5 step routine is not necessary. Sometimes minimal is also sufficient. Be gentle with your skin.
